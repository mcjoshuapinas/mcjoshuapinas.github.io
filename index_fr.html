PhD. Joshua Piñas

I have a Ph.D in Engineering Science from Paris Est Creteil University. While my Ph.D journay, I have developed an innovative method to identify the meteorological sequence that makes the indoor temperature of homes inadequate.

I have a Master's Degree in Physics. The research was developed with the collaboration between CERTES (Université Paris-Est Créteil), France, and UNI, Peru.

I have a wide knowledge of the thermal, mechanical, and other properties of solid construction materials.

In my master thesis, I used COMSOL MULTIPHYSICS to evaluate, model and make simulations of LAMBDA-UNI, Portable LAMBDA-UNI, and Portable LAMBDA-CERTES. Besides, I've been involucred in the process of the patent Portable LAMBDA-UNI.

Finally, I got the grade of physical engineering with the study of thermal, mechanical, morphological, and structural of the adobe as a biocomposite material. Also, I started the design and construction of LAMBDA-UNI according to ASTM norms: C177 and C1043.
