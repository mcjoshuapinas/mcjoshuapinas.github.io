Research

Currently, I am working on a study comparing experimental measurements (temperature, humidity, electrical power) with simulated measurements.

To do this, we have set up a test laboratory on the UPEC central campus.

In addition, I am working on a journal article on modeling, simulation, and reduction methods at different scales: material, wall, and building.

Finally, I am interested in building management to deal with overheating periods.
